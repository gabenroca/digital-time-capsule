My trip to Japan with my older sister was everything I could imagine and more. We stayed at the Grandbell Hotel in Shinjuku and used the JR Train to get around. The culture was beautiful, food was amazing, and the people were simply the kindest. I loved Shibuya Crossing because one of my favorite movies, "Lost In Translation" was filmed there. As for food, Ichiran had delicous ramen that just melts in your mouth.
